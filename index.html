About Everest Academy

Discover a learning environment where academic excellence is combined with creativity, innovation, and a strong foundation for lifelong success.

Everest Academy is committed to delivering high-quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity and personal growth. Our approach focuses on creating confident learners who are prepared for both academic success and life beyond the classroom.

Message from our Leaders

Dear Students, Parents, and Visitors,

Welcome to Panga Secondary School. Our mission is to create a learning environment where every student feels supported and motivated to reach their full potential. We believe education is not just about textbooks but about building character, curiosity, and confidence.

Together, we strive to prepare our students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Rajan Babu Oli

Why Choose Everest Academy?

Experienced Teachers

Our experienced teachers provide strong academic guidance while encouraging curiosity, confidence, and active participation in every classroom session.

Modern Learning

We use modern teaching methods, interactive lessons, and digital tools to create an engaging learning experience that improves understanding and interest.

Holistic Development

Safe Environment
